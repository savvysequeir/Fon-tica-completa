II. Explicación

2.1 Estructura

Subject + will / shall + have + past participle + complement.

She will have finished the report by tomorrow.
We shall have completed the project by Friday.

2.2 Positiva

Usa will have con todos los sujetos. También puedes usar shall have con I y we en estilo formal.

I shall have studied by noon.
They will have arrived by six.

2.3 Negativa

Coloca not después de will/shall: will not have o won't have.

He will not have finished.
We won't have left by then.

2.4 Interrogativa

Para preguntas, coloca will o shall al inicio.

Will she have arrived by 8?
Shall we have completed it by Friday?

2.5 Usos

  • Acción terminada antes de un momento futuro.
  • Resultado futuro visto como completado.
  • Metas, planes y fechas límite.

2.6 Marcadores y ejemplos

By tomorrow, by next week, by 2030, before class, by the time...

By the time you arrive, I will have cleaned the room.
By next month, they will have learned 300 verbs.
