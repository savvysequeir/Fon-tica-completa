II. Explicación

2.1 Estructura

Sujeto + have / has + been + verbo -ing + complemento

Se usa have con I, you, we, they; y has con he, she, it.

2.2 Positiva

She has been studying English.

Reglas de -ing: make → making; run → running; die → dying; play → playing.

2.3 Negativa

They have not been working today.

La negación se coloca después de have/has: haven't / hasn't been + -ing.

2.4 Interrogativa

Have you been practicing?

La pregunta inicia con Have/Has, luego sujeto, luego been y el verbo en -ing.

2.5 Usos

Sirve para acciones que iniciaron en el pasado y continúan, acciones repetidas recientemente, o situaciones con evidencia actual.

2.6 Ejemplos

I have been reading.She has not been sleeping.Have they been working?
