II. Explicación

2.1 Estructura

Sujeto + had + been + verbo -ing + complemento

Had se usa con todos los sujetos: I, you, he, she, it, we, they.

2.2 Positiva

She had been studying English.

Reglas de -ing: make → making; run → running; die → dying; play → playing; get → getting.

2.3 Negativa

They had not been working before class.

La negación se coloca después de had: had not / hadn't + been + -ing.

2.4 Interrogativa

Had you been practicing?

La pregunta inicia con Had, luego sujeto, luego been y el verbo en -ing.

2.5 Usos

Sirve para explicar duración, causa o proceso anterior a otro momento pasado: She was tired because she had been working.

2.6 Ejemplos

I had been reading.She had not been sleeping.Had they been working?
