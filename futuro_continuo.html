II. Explicación

2.1 Estructuras

Positiva: Subject + will/shall + be + verb-ing + complement.
Negativa: Subject + will/shall + not + be + verb-ing + complement.
Interrogativa: Will/Shall + subject + be + verb-ing + complement?
Con phrasal verbs: Subject + will/shall + be + verb-ing + particle/preposition + complement.

2.2 Positiva: will/shall + be + -ing

El futuro continuo expresa una acción que estará en progreso en un momento futuro. Will puede usarse con todos los sujetos. Shall se usa principalmente con I y we en contextos formales o tradicionales.

  • I will/shall be studying tomorrow.
  • She will be working at 8:00.
  • We shall be going over the lesson.

Reglas para añadir -ing

  • Regla general: work → working.
  • Verbos terminados en -e: se elimina la e. close → closing.
  • Verbos terminados en -ie: cambia ie por y. lie → lying.
  • Una sílaba CVC: se duplica la consonante final. run → running.
  • Verbos terminados en -y: se conserva la y. study → studying.

Verbos que normalmente no se usan con -ing

Algunos verbos expresan estados, posesión, pensamiento, gusto o percepción. Normalmente se prefieren en formas simples, no continuas.

  • Mente: know, understand, believe, remember.
  • Deseo/gusto: want, need, prefer, like, love, hate.
  • Posesión: have, own, belong.
  • Sentidos: see, hear, smell, taste.
  • Estado: seem, mean, cost.

Algunos pueden usarse con -ing si cambian de significado: I will be thinking about it, She will be having lunch.

2.3 Negativa

Subject + will/shall + not + be + verb-ing + complement.
  • They will not be taking off their shoes.
  • We shall not be going back home.
  • She will not be checking the messages.

2.4 Interrogative

Will/Shall + subject + be + verb-ing + complement?
  • Will you be turning on the TV?
  • Shall we be switching on the radio?
  • Will he be choosing the topic?

2.5 Usos

  • Acción en progreso en un momento futuro: At 8:00, she will be working.
  • Planes o acciones esperadas: We shall be meeting tomorrow.
  • Pregunta cortés sobre planes: Will you be using the computer?
  • Acciones futuras con phrasal verbs: They will be cleaning up the classroom.

2.6 Ejemplos

  • I will be waking up at six tomorrow.
  • We shall be finding out the answer.
  • Will you be getting off at the next stop?
  • She will not be admiring the landscape.
  • They will be connecting the cables.
